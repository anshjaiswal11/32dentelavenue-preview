Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our practice and services.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes — we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will help verify benefits and provide transparent estimates.

What financing options are available?

We offer payment plans and work with third-party financing partners to make treatment affordable. Ask our front desk for details.

Are your procedures painful?

We prioritize comfort and use modern anesthesia and sedation options to minimize discomfort. Your clinician will discuss options with you.

How long does recovery take after oral surgery?

Recovery varies by procedure but most patients return to normal activities within a few days. We provide clear aftercare instructions and support.

Do you offer emergency appointments?

Yes — we reserve time each day for urgent care. Call us and we will do our best to see you promptly.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring a photo ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any recent dental records or X-rays. Arrive a few minutes early to complete registration.
